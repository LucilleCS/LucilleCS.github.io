About the owner

I'm Maria Sue, and I'm the proud owner of pauws for beans. Get it, pause/paws for beans (coffee beans). I wanted to create a safe and cozy space for people and cats to get along.
After having my own childhood cat, sprinkles, I wished people could enjoy a cats company more than anything. I hope you stop by and enjoy this cafe as much as I think sprinkles would have!
